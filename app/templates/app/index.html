How to Use This Site:


User Type: Requestor

Request a bug, new feature, process change, etc.

To make a request, just log into the site through your google or facebook account. The website uses OAuth to collect your email address and name, and it creates a user instance for you. To review status changes on your request, you can open the "Bug Workqueue" page and click on your request.
